About Wellar AI

We build operational wellness intelligence for modern teams.

Wellar AI helps organizations run daily wellness scans, track cognitive fatigue, and act on real-time insights before incidents happen. We focus on transport, factories, clinics, and any workforce that needs reliable, continuous health signals. Our platform blends mobile scan workflows with enterprise reporting so leaders can make faster, safer decisions.

Mission

Reduce operational risk with quick mobile scans, clear dashboards, and enterprise-ready reporting that managers can trust.

What we deliver

Daily scan prompts, instant insight summaries, and role-based reporting across departments. Teams can track trends, investigate incidents, and document compliance.

Who we serve

Transport fleets, factories, logistics, clinics, and public services that need dependable wellness signals and consistent safety workflows.

How the platform works

Employees complete a short guided scan on mobile. Managers view insights in the web dashboard, track daily coverage, and act on alerts before risk escalates. Everything is logged for audit readiness and operational visibility.

Platform stewardship

The Wellar AI team designs the product, monitors infrastructure, and supports onboarding for every organization. We prioritize reliability, privacy, and actionable reporting at scale.
