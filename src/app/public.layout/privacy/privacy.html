Privacy

We protect employee data with enterprise standards.

Wellar AI stores only the data required to deliver wellness insights. We encrypt sensitive data in transit and at rest, and we never sell employee information. Our goal is to keep scans useful without collecting unnecessary personal data.

Data minimization

We collect scan results, timestamps, and organization references needed for reporting and compliance workflows.

Access controls

Role-based permissions ensure only authorized managers can access team-level reports. Employees only see their own data.

Audit trails

Invites, requests, and scan records are logged with timestamps for accountability and operational review.

Data use principles

Purpose-limited

Data is used for safety insights and compliance reporting only.

Retention control

Organizations can define retention periods that match local regulations.

Transparency

We help teams communicate scan goals and usage expectations to staff.
